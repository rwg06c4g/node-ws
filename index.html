Environmental Status

Understanding the environmental challenges we face helps us take better action

Global Warming

Over the past 100 years, the average global temperature has risen by about 1.1°C, causing glacial melting and sea level rise.

78% increase in carbon emissions since 1970

Water Pollution

More than 80% of wastewater flows back into the ecosystem without being treated, polluting rivers, lakes and oceans.

83% of global wastewater remains untreated

Deforestation

About 18 million acres of forests are lost each year, equivalent to 27 soccer fields every minute.

65% of original forests already destroyed

Take Action

Small changes in daily life can make a big difference to our planet

Use reusable bags, bottles and containers. Avoid single-use plastics like straws and utensils.

Save Energy
